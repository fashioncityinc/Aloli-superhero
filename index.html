About Mulan

Mulan is a young chinese woman who decides to enlist in the chinese war in place of her elderly and frail father since her only brother is a little baby.
She cuts her hair totally transforming into a man and goes off to the training camp where she successfully passes off as a man, makes new friends and improves her fighting skills, with the help of her guardian angel, a small dragon called Mushu.
She, with the help from her newly made soldier friends help win the war.

Fun facts about Mulan

  1. Mulan is based on a folklore about a young maiden Fa Mulan with a similar story
  2. According to the folklore, Mulan can already fight
  3. Mulan helps rescue her country with the help of her friends
  4. In real life, there is no Mushu
